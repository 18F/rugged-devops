title: Cryptography 101
---

- "math is hard"
- no hand-rolled crypto, use a library
- random numbers: urandom
- passwords: bcrypt
- hashing secrets: HMAC-SHA256
- Signing: HMAC-SHA256
- Storing/transmitting data (sym): Fernet
- Anything else: freak out and ask for help
